Color Matching Requests

Need help finding your perfect shade? Email us a photo of your natural hair taken in natural lighting (near a window or outdoors). For the best match, include:

  • A clear photo of your hair from roots to ends
  • Whether your hair is color-treated or natural
  • Your desired length and texture
  • Any inspiration photos you have in mind

We'll review your photos and recommend the best match within 24 hours.
