Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for items that are unopened and in their original packaging. To be eligible, the product must be in the same condition you received it — sealed, unused, and with all original tags intact.

Hygiene Policy

For hygiene reasons, hair extensions that have been opened, tried on, or installed are FINAL SALE. We cannot accept returns or issue refunds for any hair product that has been removed from its sealed packaging. No exceptions. This policy exists to protect the health and safety of all our customers.

Color Matching

If you open your extensions and find the color doesn't match, contact us within 48 hours of opening at alex@dropdeadhair.com. We offer a one-time exchange for a different shade. Please include a photo of the extensions next to your natural hair in natural lighting. Opened color-matched items cannot be returned for a refund — exchange only.

How to Return

To initiate a return, follow these steps:

  • Email alex@dropdeadhair.com with your order number and reason for return.
  • We will send you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours.
  • Pack the item securely in its original packaging and ship it back.
  • Once we receive and inspect the return, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to your original payment method. Please allow additional time for your bank or credit card provider to process the refund. Original shipping costs are non-refundable.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for unopened items within 30 days of delivery. For color exchanges, you must contact us within 48 hours of opening the product. Exchanges are subject to availability. If the requested item is out of stock, we will issue a store credit.
