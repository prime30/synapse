Information We Collect

When you place an order or interact with our store, we may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment providers)
  • Order history and product preferences
  • Color matching: Photos of your natural hair, texture preferences, and desired length

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Fulfill and ship your orders
  • Provide personalized color matching consultations
  • Send marketing communications (only with your consent)
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Improve our products and website experience

Color Matching Photos

Photos submitted for color matching are stored securely and used solely for the purpose of matching you to the right shade. All color matching photos are permanently deleted within 90 days of your order being completed. We never share these photos with third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our business, including Shopify (e-commerce platform), payment processors (Shopify Payments, PayPal), and shipping carriers (USPS, UPS). These providers only access the data necessary to perform their services. We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for Shopify analytics and, with your consent, marketing pixels to show you relevant ads. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.
