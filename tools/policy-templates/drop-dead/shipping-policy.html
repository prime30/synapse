Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days after payment is confirmed. Custom color orders require additional preparation and are processed within 3–5 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will begin processing the next business day.

Shipping Methods

We offer the following domestic shipping options:

  • USPS Priority Mail: 2–3 business days after processing
  • UPS Ground: 3–5 business days after processing
  • Expedited shipping: Available at checkout for faster delivery

Delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed. Delays may occur due to carrier issues, weather, or high order volume.

Free Shipping

Enjoy free standard shipping on all domestic US orders over $150. Free shipping applies to USPS Priority Mail and is calculated after any discounts are applied.

International Shipping

We ship internationally to select countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination and are calculated at checkout. Customers are responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and import fees imposed by their country. Drop Dead is not responsible for delays caused by customs processing.

Order Tracking

A tracking number will be emailed to you as soon as your order ships. You can use this number to track your package through the carrier's website. If you do not receive a tracking email within 5 business days of placing your order, please contact us at alex@dropdeadhair.com.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged or does not arrive at all, please contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date at alex@dropdeadhair.com. Include your order number and any photos of damage. We will file a claim with the carrier and either reship your order or issue a full refund.
